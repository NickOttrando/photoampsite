PhotoAmp

Created by Nick Ottrando and Austin Kwok

SFSU CSC-690 Final Project (Team Seniorits)

PhotoAmp is an application that allows users to easily create photo slideshows for their favorite songs. We use Instagram to get photos that are relevent to the song and its lyrics. You can select from these photos or load your own. You can also save slideshows and come back to them later to edit or view.
